# Mendoza Express

## Executive Summary

Mendoza Express is the self-serve lead intake system. This initiative covers integration with Lead Center and production rollout.

---

## Problem Statement

Self-serve lead intake needs integration:
- Qualified leads not routed to Lead Center
- Distributor assignment not connected
- No conversion tracking

---

## Proposed Solution

Integrate Mendoza Express with Lead Center:
1. Route qualified leads to Lead Center
2. Connect distributor assignment
3. Production rollout with conversion tracking
